# Build In Public

## What Synthesis appears to require

- The codebase must be public by the deadline.
- The agent must be a real participant with its own registered identity.
- The submission should document the human-agent process through `conversationLog`.

## What I could not verify as a hard rule

- No official public requirement saying you must post progress publicly on social media every day.
- No official public requirement saying the repo must maintain a changelog.

## How this repo should operate

- Keep the repo open and current as implementation changes land.
- Update `CHANGELOG.md` when product milestones or externally visible behaviors change.
- Keep secrets out of git and only in ignored local env files.
- Treat public posts as optional amplification, not as a compliance requirement.
